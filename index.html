Pryme

Plug-and-Play Blockchain in 60 Seconds

Universal, Lightweight, Rust-Native

PrymeChain is a self-sustaining, CLI-driven blockchain you can spin up in under one minute—no cloud, no manual config, zero hidden dependencies.

Why PrymeChain?

  • Instant Onboarding – download one binary, create a wallet, register as a validator in 60s.
  • Enterprise-Grade Security – Ed448-Goldilocks signatures + BLAKE3 hashing, backed by Rust’s safety.
  • Eco-Efficient Rewards – fixed 60 s block cadence and halving schedule use <1% of a PoW chain’s energy.

How It Works

Every menu option in Pryme’s CLI directly invokes a Rust module: database setup, wallet gen, node registration, P2P consensus, audit exports (JSON/PDF with BLAKE3 proofs) and more—each step fully transparent and zero-config.
