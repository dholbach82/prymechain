Pryme

Blockchain in 60 Seconds

Pryme "prime" is a self-sustaining, CLI-driven blockchain you can spin up and perform all it's operations in under one minute—no cloud, no manual config, zero hidden dependencies.

Why Pryme Blockchain?

  • Instant Onboarding – download one binary, create a wallet, register as a validator in 60 s.
  • Built-in Audit System – export transaction logs in JSON or PDF with BLAKE3 fingerprints.
  • Enterprise-Grade Security – Ed448-Goldilocks signatures + BLAKE3 hashing.
  • Rust-Powered – 100 % written in Rust, leveraging its memory-safety guarantees.
  • Eco-Efficient Rewards – fixed 60 s block cadence uses <1 % of a PoW chain’s energy.
  • 300 M Max Supply – 200 M pre-minted + 100 M for validator rewards.

How It Works

Every menu option in Pryme’s CLI directly invokes a Rust module: database setup, wallet generation, node registration, P2P consensus, audit exports (JSON/PDF with BLAKE3 proofs) and more—each step fully transparent and zero-config.
